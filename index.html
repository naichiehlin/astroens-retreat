Travel & Lodging

Everything you need to get there and settle in. Questions? Email the address at the bottom.

Getting there

The venue sits on the eastern shore of Lake Annecy, about 45 minutes from the city by road.

  • Nearest station Annecy (SNCF)
  • Nearest airport Geneva (GVA)
  • Group shuttle Thu 13:00
  • Parking on-site Free

Where you'll stay

Shared lodge rooms (2 per room) with breakfast included. Linens provided.

  • Check-in Thu from 14:00
  • Check-out Sat by 11:00
  • Wi-Fi Throughout
  • Cost Covered by lab

What to bring

It's the mountains in autumn — pack layers and something for the lake.

  • Walking shoes Essential
  • Swimwear Optional
  • Laptop For talks
  • One unfinished idea Required
