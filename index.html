Getting There

Gîte Auberge Le Chamois sits at the top of the Col d'Ornon, on the route du Taillefer in the Oisans.

The venue

A mountain gîte with 7 rooms sleeping up to 25 — run by Camille, Sarah & Théo since 2022.

  • Altitude Col d'Ornon
  • Rooms 7 · dorms + doubles
  • Board Self-catered
Open in Google Maps →

The drive

Roughly 1h45 from Lyon, heading toward Bourg-d'Oisans then up the col. Carpools leave together.

  • From ENS de Lyon
  • Departs Tue 09:00
  • Distance ~140 km
  • Returns Thu 15:00

What to bring

It's the mountains in June — warm in the sun, cold once it drops behind the ridge.

  • Walking shoes Essential
  • Warm layers Yes
  • Your talk 20 min
  • Competitive spirit Required
