How the weighting works

Teams have different sizes (6, 5 and 4 members), so a raw average would let bigger teams dominate the jury. Instead, each ballot is weighted by 1 / (size of the voter's team), so every team carries exactly the same total voting power, no matter how many of its members vote. A team's score in each course is the weighted average of all ballots it received; the total is the sum over the four courses (max 8).
